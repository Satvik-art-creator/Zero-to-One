# Place Bridge
## Problem Statement:
### The campus placement process is often the most critical yet stressful period for a student. Despite its importance, the current workflow in most institutions suffers from several systemic inefficiencies:

-Fragmented Communication: Critical placement updates, schedule changes, and links are often scattered across emails, WhatsApp groups, and physical notice boards, leading to missed deadlines and information decay.

-Lack of Real-time Tracking: There is no centralized dashboard for students to monitor their application status (e.g., Applied, Shortlisted, Interviewed), leading to transparency gaps and increased student anxiety.

-Data Inconsistency: Academic records and skill sets are not dynamically linked to job applications, often resulting in students applying with outdated information or failing to meet rapidly changing company requirements.

## Proposed Solution:
- PlaceBridge is a centralized, web-based recruitment hub designed to automate the match-making process between students and campus placement drives. By integrating a real-time eligibility engine, it eliminates manual screening and ensures that students only see and apply for roles they are qualified for, based on their CGPA, skills, and academic standing.
